About Us

As a Triple Board-Certified and Reconstructive Surgeon who holds Fellowship of the South African College, the Royal College of Surgeons in England (FRCS), and the European Board, Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgeons (FEBOPRAS), Dr Jali is an exceptionally skilled surgeon in Johannesburg, with immense compassion for his patients. The best surgery is that which combines a modern approach with conservative principles resulting in rapid recovery whilst maintaining and refining the beautiful normal.
